How to use these complete scenes:
1. Click "View 3D + Download" to preview the scene in Sketchfab's 3D viewer
2. Download as GLTF/GLB (Sketchfab's default export format)
3. Load the whole GLB into R3F with a single <primitive object={gltf.scene} />
4. You can also break apart the scene in Blender to extract individual buildings

Drew's Quick-Pick Recommendations:

For best style match with your existing KayKit assets:
• KayKit Medieval Hexagon — 200+ pieces, CC0, GLTF, same art style. Includes tavern, church, blacksmith, windmill, market.
• KayKit City Builder Bits — Already owned! Use these as-is for modern town areas.

For maximum variety:
• Kenney Fantasy Town Kit — 160 assets, CC0. Biggest free fantasy town pack available. Freshly remade in 2025.
• Kenney Medieval Town Base — 65 assets, CC0. Good complement to Fantasy Town Kit.

For quick wins (individual buildings):
• Poly Pizza — Search + download specific GLTF models instantly. No login needed.
• Sketchfab — Preview in 3D before downloading. Check license per model.

Art style notes: KayKit uses a single gradient atlas texture. Kenney uses flat vertex colors. Quaternius is somewhere in between. Mixing KayKit + Kenney works if you keep them in separate areas of the town (e.g., KayKit for the main square, Kenney for the outskirts).
