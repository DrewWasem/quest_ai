## Animation Clip Quick Reference (139 total)

### Best for Comedy Scenes
- **Celebrations:** Cheering, Waving, Jump_Full_Short, Jump_Full_Long
- **Confusion:** Hit_A, Hit_B, Walking_Backwards, Idle_B
- **Physical Comedy:** Throw, Melee_2H_Attack_Spin, Jump_Full_Long, Dodge_Forward
- **Working:** Work_A, Hammering, Chop, Dig, Fishing_Cast
- **Social:** Sit_Chair_Down, Sit_Chair_Idle, Sit_Floor_Down, Lie_Down
- **Skeleton Specials:** Skeletons_Taunt, Skeletons_Death_Resurrect, Skeletons_Awaken_Floor
- **Magic:** Ranged_Magic_Spellcasting, Ranged_Magic_Summon, Ranged_Magic_Raise
- **Stealth:** Sneaking, Crouching, Lockpick

### Animations to Avoid in Comedy Scenes
- Death_A, Death_B (too serious for party/picnic contexts)
- Death_A_Pose, Death_B_Pose (too still, not funny)
- Combat clips in non-combat contexts (UNLESS used for comedy — barbarian "decorating")
